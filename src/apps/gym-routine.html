Five days. One rhythm.

A five-day beginner hypertrophy program built around the home gym. Pick the day, keep the cues close, and add one clean rep before adding weight.

Split Push / Pull / Legs / Upper / Lower
Rest 2-3 min compounds · 60-90 sec isolation
Target Add 1 rep or a small load weekly
Today D1 Push

Heavy pressing, shoulder volume, and triceps with safe solo setup.

Weekly Sets 83

Three heavy sessions and two lighter volume days across the week.

Session Length 55-75 min

Heavy days run longer because compounds get full rest.

Rule 1-2 RIR

Work hard, but stop while bar speed and form are still clean.

Readiness Checklist

Warm up every movementTake 2 light movement sets before each working weight—movement, not stretching.
Log every working setIf you do not track it, you lose the progression engine.
Volume days stay lighterThe plan only works if day 4 and day 5 leave recovery intact.
Technical reps onlyStop when a rep would turn ugly. That is the real autoregulation line.
